Projects

Koos Zuilhof was part of the Dutch National Youth Jazz Orchestra and the Jong Metropole Orchestra from 2014 till 2018. Besides that he played in the Big Bands of the Royal Conservatory of The Hague, Codart Conservatory in Rotterdam and the Utrecht's Conservatory Big Band multiple times. In 2014 he played with the band Hunting The Robot, whom where 3FM talent at that time and played at Eurosonic Noordslag Festival and Paradiso Amsterdam. Around the year 2014 Koos was based in the Kytopia Studios in Utrecht were he was coached as a composer in a young talent project, he witnessed the live studio recording of Snarky Puppy's album 'We Like It Here'.
