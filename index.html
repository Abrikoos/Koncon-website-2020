Biography

Koos Zuilhof was born in 1996 in Utrecht, The Netherlands. He started playing the saxophone from an early age. He did a Bachelor Jazz Saxophone at the Royal Conservatory of The Hague from 2014 till 2018. Over the past few years his teachers have been John Ruocco, Rolf Delfos, Toon Roos, Marc Scholten, Simon Rigter, Jarmo Hoogendijk and Eric Ineke. During the four years of his studie at the Royal Conservatory he played in the National Youth Jazz Orchestra of the Netherlands. This orchestra was conducted by Martin Fondse and Anton Goudsmit. Apart from touring The Netherlands they did a tour in England with concerts in Nottingham, London and Newcastle. Koos was part of the Jong Metropole Orchestra in 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018.
